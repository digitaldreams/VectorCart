# Lifestyle & Romance Dataset

## Overview
Created 100 tasteful products focused on romance, relationships, wellness, leisure activities, and intimate moments for couples. This dataset tests semantic search in the lifestyle and relationship domain.

## Dataset Philosophy
Products are carefully curated to represent:
- **Healthy relationships**: Communication, bonding, shared experiences
- **Wellness & self-care**: Spa, relaxation, mindfulness
- **Quality time**: Date nights, activities, adventures
- **Comfort & luxury**: Sleep, relaxation, pampering
- **Memories & connection**: Keepsakes, personalization, meaningful gifts

- **Married/Long-term**: Anniversary gifts, renewal experiences, keepsakes

### 2. Price Tiers
- **Affordable** ($19.99 - $49.99): Candles, games, small gifts

- **Premium** ($159.99 - $449.99): Luxury bedding, major experiences
- **Luxury** ($599.99 - $899.99): Retreats, tandem bikes, getaway packages

### 3. Experience vs Physical Products
- **Physical Products** (70%): Items you can touch, use, keep

## Cross-Domain Testing

### Should Match Across Domains

1. **"organic natural products"**
   - Lifestyle: Organic skincare, natural aromatherapy
   - Groceries: Organic foods
   - Clothing: Organic fabrics

2. **"luxury premium items"**
   - Lifestyle: Silk bedding, spa experiences
   - Tech: Premium laptops
   - Groceries: Gourmet foods

3. **"wellness and health"**
   - Lifestyle: Spa, meditation, fitness trackers
   - Groceries: Healthy foods, supplements
   - Clothing: Athletic wear

### Should NOT Match

1. **"romantic"** → Should only match lifestyle, not tech or groceries
2. **"massage"** → Should match lifestyle wellness, not unrelated products
3. **"couples"** → Should specifically match lifestyle products designed for two
